# About Me | Luminal Works

Welcome to **Luminal Works**. This is a **solo venture** led by a dedicated **music producer and musician** with a passion for high-end digital architecture. I specialize in bridging the gap between professional music production and innovative software development.

### The Vision
Luminal Works explores the synergy between sound and code, focusing on:
* **Digital Audio Tools:** Developing unique plugins designed to solve specific creative hurdles in the mix.
* **Vintage Emulation:** Reimagining the character and warmth of classic analog gear through precise digital modeling.
* **Aesthetic Utility:** Crafting visual interfaces that make complex audio processing intuitive and inspiring.

#### Current Projects
* **[grabovoi-app](https://github.com/Luminal-Works/grabovoi-app):** Harmonizing numerical sequences with mobile utility.
* **Lumina-Press:** Vintage Motown-style compressor with M/S processing and Inter-Plugin Sync.
* **Lumina-Q:** Professional Master EQ designed to communicate with Lumina-Press for dynamic carving.

*Building the future of sound and visual intelligence.*
